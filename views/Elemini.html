What is Element?

The Element Conference is UVSASE’S premier leadership conference aimed to provide professional and personal development for aspiring young leaders of the Vietnamese-American community. Our programs, spanning across three days and two nights, are designed to provide participants with the knowledge to excel in their leadership roles and careers. Through an immersive learning environment, this conference will provide the hands-on experience for attendees to learn the skills needed to be successful in all their endeavors.



Theme?

Do you feel like you are stuck in the dirt? Many of us were put under amount of stress during the last year where different areas of our lives were impacted. Whether it was adjusting to changed pace of school and/or work, you may have struggled through it. You may not know what kind of potential you hold that can be developed. You know you have a lot of potential to grow but don’t have the right tools? Everyone is unique in their own way and there no concrete path to be successful leader. Imagine that everyone is seed that has the potential to develop and grow. Not everyone grows exactly the same whether as a potato or tomato BUT eventually finds their own niche in the garden. That means everyone may utilize different tools to get where they want to be as a leader or in a career.
